Frequently Asked Questions

For Insurers

Why should your fund be on HaltH?
HaltH is purpose-built to help private health insurers thrive in a digital-first Allied Health ecosystem. By joining the platform, your fund can unlock transformative benefits across claims, engagement, insights, and brand positioning — all while reducing administrative overhead.
Does HaltH cost more than what insurers pay today to process claims?
No — for standard claim processing, there is no additional cost beyond what insurers already pay today. For insurers looking to go further, HaltH offers a suite of optional advanced features that unlock deeper visibility, smarter automation, and greater member impact.
How do we integrate?
HaltH connects to all major private health insurers through an existing middleware platform that's already integrated with the insurer ecosystem. This allows us to offer real-time eligibility checks, digital claim submission, and secure data exchange — with minimal lift on your side.
How is fraud minimized?
HaltH is designed from the ground up to reduce fraud, leakage, and misuse in the Allied Health claims process. Fraud prevention is embedded at multiple levels including verified member data, Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), provider identity verification, and automated rules-based claim submission.
What data do we get?
As a health fund on HaltH, you get access to transparent, real-time data at the member level including booking and attendance data, claim activity and payment flows, member engagement signals, out-of-pocket insights, and aggregated usage trends.
What health modalities are supported at launch?
HaltH is launching with a strong focus on Australia's most in-demand, health fund–supported Allied Health services including Physiotherapy, Psychology, Podiatry, Speech Pathology, Occupational Therapy, Osteopathy, Chiropractic, Dietetics, Exercise Physiology, and Optometry.

General

What is HaltH?
HaltH is a platform for online bookings, payments, and health fund claims, purpose-built for Allied Health in Australia. It connects members, providers, and private health insurers, making the entire experience — from appointment to reimbursement — easier, faster, and more transparent. All transactions on HaltH are powered by secure, best-in-class payment infrastructure that protects health and financial data to the highest global standards. We work closely with Australia’s leading health insurers, connecting through secure APIs developed either by our team or via trusted funding partners. This enables real-time eligibility checks, automatic claim submissions, and seamless, paperless experiences for all users.
Who is HaltH for?
HaltH is for everyone involved in the Allied Health ecosystem — from clients (members) booking care, to practice and clinic owners (providers) delivering it, and private health insurers (PHIs) helping fund it. Each group benefits from a more seamless, transparent experience.
How does it work?
Members book appointments, know their health fund support upfront, and handle payments digitally. Providers get bookings with reduced admin, and insurers connect for real-time, verified funding. We're a global leader in secure payments, to power all transactions on the HaltH platform.
Where is HaltH available?
HaltH is currently available to Allied Health providers and their clients (members) across Australia. We're focused on building a strong national network, with initial traction in metro and regional areas where demand for accessible, funded care is high.
Does HaltH store any health data?
No — HaltH does not store any medical or clinical health data. We're not a PMS, medical record system, or clinical app. Our platform is designed purely to manage bookings, payments, and health fund claims — and to do so securely. We store only the minimum personal information required to verify a booking, process a claim, or facilitate payment.
How safe and secure is HaltH?
Extremely. HaltH is secure by design and engineered to meet — and exceed — the highest global compliance standards in healthcare, payments, and data privacy. We use end-to-end encryption, tokenized payment data, and strict role-based access controls to protect member and provider information at every step.
Is HaltH compliant with privacy laws?
Yes — privacy is fundamental to how HaltH operates. We comply with all relevant Australian privacy laws, including the Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), which govern the handling of personal and health information.

For Members

Do I need to be a HaltH customer to make a booking?
Nope — you don't need to sign up or create an account to make a booking. Just enter your health fund and payment details when prompted, and you're good to go. It's quick, secure, and fully digital — designed to get you booked in as quick as possible.
Can I claim and pay without creating an account?
Yes — you don't need to create a HaltH account to claim or pay. Whether you're booking in advance or settling up after your appointment, you can simply enter your health fund and payment details to complete the process.
When will I be asked for my health fund details?
HaltH will prompt you to enter your health fund details at key moments — like when you're booking an appointment, making a payment, or managing your profile within the app. We only ask when it's needed to confirm your eligibility and help process your claim.
How do I know how much of my appointment is covered?
Once you enter your health fund details, HaltH instantly shows you an upfront estimate of how much your fund is likely to cover — and what, if anything, you'll need to pay. It's all about transparency, so you can book with confidence and avoid unexpected costs.
What are the benefits of using the HaltH app?
The HaltH app puts everything you need to manage your Allied Health care in one place. You can book and reschedule appointments, add and update payment methods, track how much funding you have left, and see your full health team in one view.
How is my private health insurance data shared?
Only what's absolutely necessary is shared — and only with the right parties. We securely share your member number, claim amount, and basic booking details with your health provider and private health insurer so your claim can be processed smoothly. That's it.
How is my payment data handled?
Your payment details are encrypted and tokenised, meaning they're converted into secure codes and never stored or shared in their raw form. We use world-class payments infrastructure to process every transaction — the same technology trusted by leading financial institutions.
Is it safe to use HaltH?
Not only do we think so, but also the growing network of providers and members. We use best-in-class payment and data protection tech to make sure your health and payment info stays safe. This ensures your financial data is protected to the highest international standards.

For Providers

What's in it for providers?
HaltH helps providers grow, streamline, and modernise their practice — all while delivering a better experience for their clients. With HaltH, providers get more bookings, fewer no-shows, faster automated payments, less admin, and happier clients who know what they're covered for before they walk in.
How do I get started?
It's easy — just sign up for free and start engaging better with your clients straight away. There are no setup fees, no terminal rentals or long contracts. You only pay once you start getting value from HaltH — typically through transaction-based fees tied to real bookings and claims.
How are payments handled?
Payments are processed automatically and sent straight to your account through HaltH's integrated payment platform — no terminals, no chasing, no gaps. Clients enter their details once, and payments are settled securely and on time after each appointment.
Does HaltH check health fund details?
Yes — HaltH verifies a member's health fund eligibility upfront by generating real-time rebate estimates or quotes before they confirm their booking. This helps ensure everyone knows what's covered and what the out-of-pocket cost will be — no surprises.
Can I integrate HaltH with my current PMS?
Yes — HaltH is designed to integrate smoothly with your existing workflows. We've already built — and continue to build — integrations with popular Practice Management Systems (PMS) and other tools providers rely on.
How do I connect my PMS to HaltH?
It takes just minutes. To connect your Practice Management System (PMS) to HaltH, simply paste your API key — usually found in your PMS settings into HaltH. That's it. No complex setup, no downtime, and no technical headaches.
